About Rosales National High School

Rosales National High School is committed to providing quality education and fostering an environment of excellence. Established in 1980, our school has grown to become one of the premier educational institutions in the region.

We believe in holistic development, focusing not just on academics but also on extra-curricular activities. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure that each student reaches their full potential.

Our vision is to produce responsible, well-rounded individuals who are ready to face the challenges of the future. We are guided by our core values of integrity, respect, and excellence in everything we do.
