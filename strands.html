Explore Strands at Rosales National High School

alt="STEM Image">
STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

The STEM strand focuses on developing skills in scientific research, technological innovations, engineering solutions, and mathematical problem-solving. Students explore subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Calculus, and advanced Biology, aiming for careers in fields like engineering, medicine, and computer science.

alt="HUMSS Image">
HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

HUMSS explores human behavior, culture, society, and history. Students engage with subjects such as Psychology, Sociology, and Literature, preparing for careers as lawyers, teachers, journalists, and social workers.

alt="TVL Image">
TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood)

TVL equips students with practical skills in specific technical fields, such as culinary arts, electronics, welding, automotive, and beauty services. It is ideal for students seeking employment or entrepreneurship after graduation.

alt="ABM Image">
ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

ABM focuses on business management, accounting, and financial decision-making. Students explore financial management, marketing, economics, and entrepreneurship, making it a great choice for those aiming for careers in business, finance, and management.
